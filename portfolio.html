Project 2-FAKE NEWS DETECTION SYSTEM

A fake news detection system is a tool or algorithm designed to identify and flag misinformation or misleading information in news articles or content. These systems typically use a combination of natural language processing, machine learning, and data analysis techniques to analyze the text, source, and context of a news article to determine its credibility. Such systems can assess various factors like the reputation of the source, the language used, the presence of sensationalism or emotional language, and cross-reference information with trusted sources to determine if a news article is likely to be accurate or not.
